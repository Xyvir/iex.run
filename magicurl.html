Please read through all the steps below at least once before performing them.

1. Click the 'Copy' button below.

2. Press Winkey and 'R' together. ( ⊞ + R ) NOTE: Windows key is bottom-left between CTRL and ALT.

3. Paste. (CTRL +V)

4. Press Enter.

5. If prompted, click 'Yes' on any 'Do you want to allow this app...' pop-up.

6. Please wait until you see the green 'Complete!' message.

7. After you see the 'Complete!' message, you are free to close all terminal windows.


NOTE: If anything goes wrong, or you are still having issues, please inform your IT department.
